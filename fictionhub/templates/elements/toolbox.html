What is the story about? Describe it's premise, the "What if?".

Write a paragraph about the world where the story takes place.

Describe a character in a situation.

Describe the event that disrupts the normal course of events and begins the story. What can go wrong? What problem is character facing? What goal does he have? Who is the antagonist?

How can things escalate? How does the character pursue his goal, what obstacles/challenes does he need to overcome? How can things spiral out of control, raising the stakes, requiring bigger and bigger risks, leading to the showdown between the hero and antagonist?

How does it end? Does the character win? How? Why?

How do things go back to normal?
