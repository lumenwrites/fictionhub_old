Submit your story!

To submit your story, send it to fictionhub.io@gmail.com or tweet it at @fictionhub. You can also just join our community and post it yourself.

All fictional stories — science fiction, fantasy, comedy, short, long, serialized, etc, are welcome. Our only rule — please submit only the stories you’re really proud of.

All accepted stories will be shared with our twitter followers. The best stories will be featured on our front page and sent to all of our subscribers in the weekly email digest.

To make it easier for us to feature your story, and to increase the amount of people who will read it, I recommend adding a beautiful illustration at the beginning of the story. Great places to find illustrations are ArtStation and DeviantArt. The best illustrations are appealing to look at, inspire curiosity, and represent the content of your story very well.

We’re looking forward to reading your stories!
