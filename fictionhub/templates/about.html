About

Hi! Welcome to our community! Our goal is to inspire people to write, and to help each other to get better at it.

I want this website to become the perfect place for people to practice, share their stories, and receive useful and friendly feedback.

Try to be nice, helpful, and encouraging when you comment on stories. Constructive critique is welcome, just make sure that it's useful to the writer.

New to writing? I recommend to start by reading this post, and then watching an awesome writing course by Brandon Sanderson.

Here you can read a series of articles on story structure(you can also learn about it from this amazing lecture.).

And this is a great guide on writing intelligent characters.

Send me a message to raymestalez@gmail.com to discuss anything related to writingstreak.

The website is in active development, and I really appreciate any questions, suggestions, feedback and bug reports.
